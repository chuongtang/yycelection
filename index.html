HOW IT WORKS

How Vote Compass Works

Getting started is easy. With no login, and no registration, you keep control of your info. All you need is a postal so we can determine your district, electorate or riding. A Vote Compass questionnaire is typically 30 questions and takes about 10 minutes to complete. Once you finish, Vote Compass will analyse your responses and compare them with the policies of the candidates.
